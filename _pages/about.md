Hello! I recently completed my Master’s degree in Computer Engineering at [Kharazmi University of Tehran](https://khu.ac.ir/en), one of Iran’s leading universities, with a GPA of 4.0/4.0 and a Master’s thesis grade of 20/20. I was advised by [Amir Asghari](https://profile.ut.ac.ir/en/~s.a.asghari) and [Mohammadreza Binesh Marvasti](https://khu.ac.ir/cv/331/Mohammadreza-Binesh-Marvasti) within the School of Engineering. I also hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering from the [University of Zanjan](https://www.znu.ac.ir/en), with a GPA of 3.27/4.0.

I have authored five manuscripts. One of them — a thesis‑related paper — was published in collaboration with the [Université de Montréal](https://www.umontreal.ca/). Another has been submitted to IEEE Access in collaboration with the [University of Innsbruck](https://www.uibk.ac.at/en/) and [Sharif University of Technology](https://en.sharif.ir/). The remaining three are currently in progress. Further details about my selected projects can be found in my [CV](https://github.com/erfanresume/erfanresume.github.io/blob/master/files/CV_ErfanMoghadam.pdf), which is available on this website. 

I currently work as a Research Assistant and a Teaching Assistant at the Data Science and Technology Group, Faculty of Intelligent Systems Engineering, [University of Tehran](https://ut.ac.ir/en). I am also collaborating on a paper with [Sharif University of Technology](https://en.sharif.ir/) and the [University of Innsbruck](https://www.uibk.ac.at/en/).

The goal of this site is to share my research, collaborate with like-minded professionals, and provide useful resources in the fields I’m passionate about.
